Jindal Steel and Power

Indian multi-national corporation

Jindal Steel and Power Limited (JSPL) is an Indian steel company based in New Delhi. JSPL is a part of OP Jindal Group.

In terms of tonnage, it is the third largest private steel producer in India and only private player in India to produce rails.

The company manufactures and sells sponge iron, mild steel slabs, rails, mild steel, structural, hot rolled plates, iron ore pellets
o ensure a cost-effective and efficient construction solution process, JSP offers products that are light-weight, eco-friendly, fire-resistant products
